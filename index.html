How to Play:

  • Objective: Win rounds by selecting the best financial metric from your company card
  • Gameplay: Each round, you'll see your company card with various financial stats
  • Your Move: Click on any stat you think is stronger than your opponent's
  • Comparison: Your chosen stat will be compared with the opponent's same stat
  • Winning: Higher values win for most stats (Revenue, Market Cap, Employees, Profit Margin, ROE, Growth)
  • Special Stats: Lower is better for P/E Ratio and Debt-to-Equity
  • Scoring: Win a round = +1 point. First to outpace your opponent wins!

Financial Metrics:

  • Revenue: Annual revenue in billions ($B)
  • Market Cap: Total market value in billions ($B)
  • Employees: Total workforce size
  • Profit Margin: Profitability percentage (%)
  • P/E Ratio: Price-to-Earnings ratio (lower is better)
  • Debt/Equity: Debt-to-Equity ratio (lower is better)
  • ROE: Return on Equity percentage (%)
  • Growth: Revenue growth percentage (%)
